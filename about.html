About

Two Tribes practices narrative cartography. We do not ask who is right. We ask how meaning is made. For each event, we lay out the dominant left and right narratives side by side, along with a narrow ground of confirmed facts and open questions. Same world. Different maps. This is not debate, dunking, or adjudication. It is a tool for orientation. A way to see what the other side believes they are responding to, even when you disagree.
